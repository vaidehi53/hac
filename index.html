  • Direct Taxes

    Direct taxation in India broadly refers to the Income Tax and Wealth tax laws which involve personal tax, corporate tax, minimum alternate tax, capital gains tax, international tax and transfer pricing. We offer services in each of the above mentioned areas of tax and regulatory compliance.

  • Audit

    Our audit and assurances service offerings are based on a complete understanding of the clients’ business specifics, industry peculiarities and the applicable laws. The audit approach is based on the compliance issues, the nature and requirement for audit, the clients’ requirement and key risk issues involved. We offer a wide range of audit and assurance.

  • Solutions

    The finance, accounting and compliance processes of businesses are getting complex and challenging with more and more regulatory pronouncements and standards coming into effect. We at HAC offer services to clients who either do not wish to create their own capabilities or are looking for competitive and professional solutions to their accounting, compliance and other related processes.

WHY CHOOSE HAC?

At HAC we strongly believe in establishing long term relationship with clients. Starting from the introductory meetings we would endeavour to make you feel the difference in quality of service that you can expect and shall receive.

To us every client is equally important and when you repose their trust in us, it becomes our bounden duty to serve you with utmost care and the highest level of professional competence.

We aren’t the largest firm nor do we have a global presence, but that works to our advantage. Which enables you to have unfettered, direct and easy access to our partners and senior members at practically all times.

Our strength lies in our versatility - over the years our knowledge bank and professional experience has grown into a formidable combination which ensures that we attend to your complex business issues from multifarious angles and provide all round solutions which are second to none.

Our team is like a family. Every member of the team is encouraged to be friendly, professional and fully committed to delivering you the best service.

At HAC we are determined to deliver success to you, because in your success lies our own success and towards that end we leave no stone unturned.
